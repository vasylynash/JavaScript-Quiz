Javascript Quiz

Welcome to the simple Javascript Quiz!

To start the quiz press the Start quiz button.

Use Next and Previous buttons to navigate between the questions. To submit your answers press the Submit button.

You can enter your initials and save the results.

On the "Highscores" page you can view the Highscores by clicking the Show highscores button. To re-start the quiz click the Start over button.

You have 30 seconds to complete the quiz. Quiz ends when you click the Submit button or when the time is over.
